Minesweeper

I created a mock-minesweeper program in C as one of my first explorations into C/C++. It works by dynamically allocating an array based on the desired grid input by the player, randomly generating values, and assigning mines to parts of that grade based on the outcome. It will continue playing until all tiles are revealed except mines. Its detection works by cascading the detection in all valid directions if there are no adjacent mines.
